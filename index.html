Hostel Location
Hostel Overview
  • Capacity & Structure: Girivar Bhawan has 351 single-seater rooms. The hostel is a four-story building divided into four blocks named A, B, C, and D.
  • Environment: The hostel features a large dining hall, a spacious common room, and a T.T. room, all surrounded by beautiful green lawns.
  • Staffing: Managed by a Hostel Supervisor and Assistant Supervisor (available 09:00 AM to 10:00 PM). Dedicated mess staff, MTW staff, one sweeper per block, and two Malis (gardeners) maintain the premises.
  • Security: 24/7 security is provided via guards at the hostel gate, night watchmen, and well-equipped CCTV camera surveillance.
Hostel Facilities & Services
  • Recreation (Indoor): A big common room equipped with a 50-inch LED TV, newspapers, magazines, Table Tennis (T.T.), Carom boards, and Chess boards.
  • Sports (Outdoor): A concrete badminton court and a volleyball ground are prepared within the hostel premises for physical activities.
  • Laundry Services: Four washing machines are installed in the hostel for convenient student use.
  • Winter Amenities: Electric Geysers are available in the washrooms on every floor to provide hot water during the winter session.
  • Convenience: Concrete benches in the lawns for relaxation and two parking sheds on both sides of the peripheral road for bicycle parking.
General Rules & Regulations
  • Strictly Prohibited: Consumption of alcohol, smoking, and any form of substance abuse are strictly prohibited inside the hostel premises.
  • Anti-Ragging: Ragging in any form is a punishable offense and will lead to immediate expulsion and disciplinary action.
  • Silence Hours: Students must maintain silence after 10:00 PM to avoid disturbing the academic environment of others.
  • Visitors: No outside guests or day-scholars are allowed inside the student rooms. Visitors must be met in the designated visitor areas or common room.
  • Electrical Appliances: Use of heavy electrical appliances like room heaters, induction cooktops, and immersion rods is strictly forbidden.
  • Cleanliness: Students must keep their rooms clean and dispose of garbage only in the designated dustbins placed in the corridors.
